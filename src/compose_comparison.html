Detailed Limitations of podman-compose

  • Partial Feature Support: Only a subset of Docker Compose features are implemented.
  • Restart Policies: Limited or partial support; may not behave as expected.
  • Health Checks: Not supported.
  • Compose v2 Syntax: Only partial support; newer features may not work.
  • Networking: Uses pod-based networking which differs from Docker's bridge mode.
  • Volume Handling: Some Docker volume options may not be available or behave differently.
  • Build Support: Build directives may require workarounds or fail.
  • Systemd Integration: Can generate systemd service files, but requires manual setup.
  • Rootless Operation: Supported natively, but may cause compatibility issues.
  • Maturity: Less mature and community-maintained; not a drop-in replacement.
  • Swarm/Orchestration: Not supported.
